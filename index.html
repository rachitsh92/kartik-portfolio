About the Advocate

Kartik Sharma

Kartik Sharma is an enrolled Advocate with the Bar Council of Delhi, practicing before the District Courts and the Delhi High Court. Over twelve years of practice, he has built a reputation for meticulous case preparation, clear client communication, and a pragmatic approach to dispute resolution — whether in the courtroom or at the negotiation table.

His practice spans civil and criminal litigation, corporate advisory, family law, property disputes, contract drafting, and cheque dishonour proceedings — giving clients a single, trusted point of contact across legal needs.

Common Questions

How do I book an initial consultation?

You can book a consultation via the WhatsApp button, the contact form below, or by calling the office directly. Initial consultations are confidential.

Which courts does Advocate Sharma practice in?

Primarily the Delhi District Courts and the Delhi High Court, with select matters before tribunals depending on case requirements.

What documents should I bring to my first meeting?

Bring any relevant agreements, notices, correspondence, identity proof and a brief written timeline of events related to your matter.

Are fees discussed upfront?

Yes. Fee structure and scope of work are discussed transparently before any engagement begins, with no hidden charges.
